Information-theoretic validation of ONT long-read data

Active

Quantifying how much usable information a raw nanopore squiggle actually carries, and using that signal to flag low-quality reads before they propagate downstream. Aim: a principled QC step grounded in information theory rather than heuristic thresholds.

ONTinfo theoryQC

Spacekime nanopore basecalling

Active

Applying the Dinov–Velev spacekime framework to ONT pod5 basecalling. Re-parameterizing squiggle-to-sequence inference with kime-phase priors with the goal of improving accuracy in low-coverage regions.

ONTsignal processingbasecalling

Designing differentially private mechanisms tailored to longitudinal signals (mood trajectories, physiological time series) that protect individual contributions without destroying clinically meaningful structure.
